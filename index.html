Bartosz Jan Bednarczyk

I am a PHD student at TU Dresden and at University of Wrocław, supervised jointly by Sebastian Rudolph (Dresden) and Emanuel Kieroński (Wrocław).
My work focus on different aspects of ontology mediated data access and applications of decidable fragments of first-order logic (like existential rules or guarded-fragments) to database-inspired reasoning problems. More broadly, I'm interested in finite model theory.

Since April 2019 I am employed in ERC grant DeciGUT. I am also a principal investigator in a Polish Ministry of Science and Higher Education grant titled "Logics with generalized counting quantifiers for data-aware processes and database systems".
